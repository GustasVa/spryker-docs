The App Orchestration Platform lets [Spryker Cloud Commerce OS (SCCOS)](/docs/cloud/dev/spryker-cloud-commerce-os/getting-started-with-the-spryker-cloud-commerce-os.html) customers to connect, configure, and use the available third-party services, or apps, in their application with a click of a button, without development efforts from their side.

![AOP](https://spryker.s3.eu-central-1.amazonaws.com/docs/aop/app-orchestration-platform-overview/aop.png)

The following apps are supported:

- PayOne, a Payment Service Provider (PSP)
- UserCentrics, a Consent Management Platform (CMP); 
- BazaarVoice, a platform for User-Generated Content (UGC)
